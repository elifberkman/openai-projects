

Recipe Title: Eggplant Parmesan Sandwich

Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 1/2 cup of milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 large eggplant

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.

2. Slice the eggplant into 1/4 inch thick slices.

3. Beat the egg in a shallow bowl.

4. Dip the eggplant slices into the egg mixture.

5. Dip the eggplant slices into the milk.

6. Place the eggplant slices on a greased baking sheet.

7. Bake for 15 minutes.

8. Toast the bread slices.

9. Place the eggplant slices on one slice of the toasted bread.

10. Top with the other slice of toasted bread.

11. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
