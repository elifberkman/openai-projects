

Recipe Title: Crispy Parmesan-Crusted Fish Fillets

Ingredients:
- 2 fish fillets 
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Place the fish fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
3. In a small bowl, combine the Parmesan cheese, paprika, and black pepper.
4. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese mixture generously over the top of the fish fillets.
5. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and the Parmesan cheese is golden and crispy.
6. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley and serve. Enjoy!
